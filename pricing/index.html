Performance Partnership Program/ 04

We get paid when you get paid. Not before.

Our pricing is structured in three layers. The base covers infrastructure. The per-appointment fee covers activity. The revenue share covers outcome. You only pay the outcome layer when an APEX-originated lead actually closes.

01Base layer

Fixed monthly base

Covers your dedicated team, infrastructure, management overhead, training, HR, payroll, and ops support. Predictable. Billed monthly. No surprises.

02Activity layer

Per-qualified-appointment fee

$100–200 per appointment that actually runs and meets your qualification criteria. Verified through your CRM, not ours. Paid monthly in arrears.

03Outcome layer

Revenue share on closed projects

5–10% of project revenue on deals that close from APEX-originated appointments. Capped per-deal at a ceiling negotiated in your contract. Tracked through CRM-attributed close events.

Why this structure

Pure retainer pricing rewards us for showing up. Pure performance pricing makes us cut corners on quality to chase volume. The three-layer model aligns us on the only thing that matters: closed revenue.

Typical client mix~60% base · ~25% appointments · ~15% revenue share

Pricing FAQ/ 06

Real answers. No "contact sales for pricing."

The $100–200 covers a qualified appointment that meets your CRM-defined criteria and actually runs (not just gets scheduled). It includes confirmation calls, reschedule handling, and pre-call data enrichment. You don't pay for no-shows, unqualified bookings, or wrong-numbers — only for appointments your sales team actually sat down with.
